# Contact Technical Support

To open a support ticket, use the Support tab this link: <https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us>. The [Logi Analytics Knowledge Base](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/categories/360004321813-Knowledge-Base) may also provide you with information for a problem you are having.

You can also contact your sales representative for assistance by emailing [salesteam@logianalytics.com](mailto:salesteam@logianalytics.com) or [emea\_sales@logianalytics.com](mailto:emea_sales@logianalytics.com).
