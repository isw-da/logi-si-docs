# Customize the Copyright Information

The copyright information appears on both the [About box](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4402963017367-About-Dialog) and the [Login screen](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4402955424535-Log-Into-the-Composer-UI). By default, standard Composer copyright information is displayed.

To customize the copyright information:

1. Log into Composer as a supervisor.
2. Select **Customize UI** on the [supervisor menu](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4402955649943-The-Composer-Supervisor-Menu) (![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4404952783767/hamburger.png)). The Customize UI page appears.
3. Under Login and About , locate the **Copyright** slider and box. To enable your custom copyright, slide the **Copyright** slider to the right (on). To disable your custom copyright, slide the **Copyright** slider to the left (off). By default the **Copyright** slider is on.

   ![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4404952916503/custom-login_576x405.png)
4. In the box below the **Copyright** slider, specify your custom copyright information.
5. Select ![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4404959543447/save-blue-btn_43x23.png) to save and apply your changes.
