# Customize the Login Screen, Home Page Background, and About Box

You can customize the copyright information, terms of service link, Login screen logo, the Login screen background, and the Home screen background. The logo, copyright, and terms of service appear in the [About box](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4402963017367-About-Dialog) of the UI. The copyright information, Login screen logo, and Login screen background appear with the Login screen. The Home screen background appears on the Home page.
