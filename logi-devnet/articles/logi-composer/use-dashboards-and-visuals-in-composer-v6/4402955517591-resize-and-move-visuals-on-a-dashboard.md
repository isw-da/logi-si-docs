# Resize and Move Visuals on a Dashboard

The dashboard canvas lets you move and rearrange the sequence of the visuals as you see fit. In addition, you can resize each individual visual.

## Move Visuals

You can arrange your visuals on the dashboard in the way that is most convenient to you to present the visuals. To move a visual to a specific place on your dashboard, grab your visual by the title pane, and drag and drop to the desired location.

## Resize Visuals

You can make the visuals on your dashboard different sizes. Depending on its type, a visual may require more space on your dashboard.

To resize a visual, select it on your dashboard and drag it by the handle in the right bottom corner. A preview border appears, showing the area that your visual occupies when moved.
