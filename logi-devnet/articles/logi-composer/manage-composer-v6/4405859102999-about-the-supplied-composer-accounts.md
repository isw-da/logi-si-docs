# About the Supplied Composer Accounts

Two unique Composer accounts are created during the Composer installation process - a *superaccount* and a *company* account.

* The *superaccount* is a dedicated and permanent account that cannot be deleted. Its sole purpose is for the maintenance of other Composer accounts and other supervisory Composer functions.

  The [supplied supervisor user](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405859125911-Supplied-User-Definitions) can only be assigned to the *superaccount*. Other users can be assigned to the *superaccount*. Users assigned to the *superaccount* have full access to the Composer supervisor UI and its supervisory functions. See [*Add and Remove Supervisors*](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405859123479-Add-and-Remove-Supervisors).
* The *company* account is the default account where the [supplied admin user](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405859125911-Supplied-User-Definitions) can perform administrator-level functions for Composer.

  Other users can be assigned as administrators of the *company* account. Simply add them to the **Administrators** group for the account.
