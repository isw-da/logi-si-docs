# Thinkspace - Adding Mark Lines

Mark Lines are lines on a chart's canvas, related to its Y-axis data values, making it easy to compare data to target values.

![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4419707182359/dm30_workcharts_49.png)

To add Mark Lines, click a Y-Axis pill's gear icon and select Mark Lines in its gear menu, as shown above.

![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4419707182615/dm30_workcharts_50.png)

Enter a value for the first line, *10000* in the example above, and click **Apply**. The resulting line and its caption are shown on the chart in the background.

![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4419707182871/dm30_workcharts_51.png)

The line's caption can be edited by clicking the "pencil" icon, making changes, and clicking **Apply**, as shown above.

![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4419707183127/dm30_workcharts_52.png)

To change the line's color, click the assigned color swatch to show the standard colors and the *Show more* link
to show even more colors. You can also enter custom color values directly as
hex ("#CCCCCC"), RGB ("rgb(197, 95, 169)"), or text ("Blue") values.

![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4419699998359/dm30_workcharts_53.png)

You can add and remove Mark Lines using the ![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4419699911063/plusminus.png) icons and even fill the area between them, as shown above.
