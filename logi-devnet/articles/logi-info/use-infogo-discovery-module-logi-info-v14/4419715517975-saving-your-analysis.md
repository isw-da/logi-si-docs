# Saving Your Analysis

The Thinkspace includes an optional feature that lets you save the
settings and visualizations you've created and re-use them in a later
session.

![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4419714911767/dm30_infogo_07_555x352.png)

If your application has been configured for this feature, the Thinkspace will display a **Save** button, as shown above. Click it to save your settings and the visualization.

![](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4419706599319/criticalicon.png) If you change the data selection, or return to the Home page, without saving your analysis, the Thinkspace will be reset and you'll lose your visualization.
