# Introducing the Designer Reporting Environment

This topic introduces the layout of the Designer reporting environment, and the functions of each part of Designer. For simple operations, it only provides a brief how-to introduction. You can get more information about the features in the linked topics.

![Designer Reporting Environment](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4420410583191/rptenvv18.2.gif)

The Designer development environment consists of the following parts:

* [Start Page](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405664702615-Start-Page)
* [Menu Tabs](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405661953175-Menu-Tabs)
* [Catalog Manager](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405661949847-Catalog-Manager)
* [Data Panel](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405661950871-Data-Panel)
* [Components Panel](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405664701079-Components-Panel)
* [Inspector Panel](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405661951895-Inspector-Panel)
* [Design/View Area](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405661948695-Design-View-Area)

![Note icon](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4420394642839/noteicon.jpg) You can dock the Data, Components, and Inspector panels into Designer or display them as floating windows. To display a panel as a floating window, move the pointer to the panel bar till it becomes a four-way arrow, then drag it out of the docking area. This is especially useful when you have multiple monitors available. To return a panel to it's docked position, drag the bar containing the tip "Drag here to dock." of the panel to the left or right side of Designer and when you see a box attached to your mouse pointer, release the mouse button and Designer docks the panel there.
