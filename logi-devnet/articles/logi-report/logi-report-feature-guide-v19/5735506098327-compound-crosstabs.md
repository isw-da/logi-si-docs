# Compound Crosstabs

Crosstabs are frequently used to analyze aggregated information in simple row and column fashion, often with a hierarchical structure. However, the business world requires analyses beyond this basic format. Logi Report supports a unique compound crosstab that enables users to place multiple crosstabs together in a flexible structure. Every group of rows and columns can have its own summaries. This makes far more sophisticated crosstab analysis possible.
