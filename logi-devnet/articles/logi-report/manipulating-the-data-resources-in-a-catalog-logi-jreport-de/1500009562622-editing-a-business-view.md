# Editing a Business View

You can edit a business view in a catalog at any time. For example, rename it, add more elements to it, edit the filters and so on. You can also delete the business view if it is no longer needed.

The following topics show more about editing a business view:

> * [Adding and Modifying Elements](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500009562642-Adding-and-Modifying-Elements)
