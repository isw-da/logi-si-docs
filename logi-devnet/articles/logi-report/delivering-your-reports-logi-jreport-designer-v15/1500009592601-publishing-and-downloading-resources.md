# Publishing and Downloading Resources

Logi JReport Designer is a 100% Java swing based report design tool. Developed page reports, web reports, library components, and catalogs can be published to run in distributed environments. You can publish your resources from Logi JReport Designer to your local computer or to Logi JReport Server. Meanwhile, reports created on Logi JReport Sever can also be downloaded to a specified folder on your local computer, and further modified in Logi JReport Designer.

The following topics discuss publishing and downloading resources:
