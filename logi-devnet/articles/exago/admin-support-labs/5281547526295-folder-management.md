# Folder Management

## Topics discussed:

* Storing reports, themes, and templates in a database
* Gaining fine-grained control over user permission settings
* Enhancing control with sharing, soft-deletion, revisioning, and backups

> ![red exclamation point](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/5432173635607/criticalicon.gif "Important")
>
> Exago *v2020.1* replaces all existing Storage Management methods, and therefore Folder Management is not recommended for new deployments as it will not be available. If you need more product information, including new purchases and upgrades, contact your Exago Customer Success Manager.

> ![light bulb](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/5432173099031/noteicon.jpg "Note")
>
> The **Folder Management** enhancement was not released in *v2018.2* as indicated in the video. It is currently unreleased and targeted for a future version.
