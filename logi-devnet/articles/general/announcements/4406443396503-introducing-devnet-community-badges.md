# Introducing DevNet Community Badges

![Logi_BadgesBanner_V3-01.png](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4406591611287/Logi_BadgesBanner_V3-01.png)

Hello Logi DevNet Community,

We are excited to announce the introduction of Logi DevNet Community Badges. With these tokens of appreciation, we aim to recognize Logi users who add value and enhance the online community experience. The release of the first 10 badges will focus on acknowledging:

* Post and comment activity in the forums
* Comments and votes on Documentation in the Knowledge Base
* Expertise around core Logi products based on community activity

We look forward to celebrating your contribution by awarding badges as outlined below.

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Logi_Icon_1stForumAnswered_V1-01.png | **1st Forum Post** | Awarded to members that submit their first forum post |
| Logi_Icon_10thForumAnswered_V1-01.png | **10th Forum Post** | Awarded to members that have submitted 10 forum posts |
| Logi_Icon_ForumGuru_V1-01.png | **Logi Forum Guru** | Awarded to members who are very active and provide informed comments on the Logi forums |
| Logi_Icon_LogiComposerNinja_V1-01.png | **Logi Composer Ninja** | Awarded to members that have shown expertise in Logi Composer |
| Logi_Icon_LogiInfoNinja_V1-01.png | **Logi Info Ninja** | Awarded to members that have shown expertise in Logi Info |
| Logi_Icon_LogiReportNinja_V1-01.png | **Logi Report Ninja** | Awarded to members that have shown expertise in Logi Report |
| Logi_Icon_BronzeContributor_V1-01.png | **Logi Bronze Contributor** | Awarded to members that have at least 10 community contributions (forums, votes, comments, etc.) |
| Logi_Icon_SilverContributor_V1-01.png | **Logi Silver Contributor** | Awarded to members that have at least 25 community contributions (forums, votes, comments, etc.) |
| Logi_Icon_GoldContributor_V1-01.png | **Logi Gold Contributor** | Awarded to members that have at least 50 community contributions (forums, votes, comments, etc.) |
| Logi_Icon_HackathonParticipant_V1-01.png | **Logi Hackathon Participants** | Awarded to members that participated in a Logi Hackathon |
