# Watch Exploring Composer Playground Webinar

We hosted a  Composer Playground Webinar on Thursday 12/09/21. You can read more about the webinar below and watch the recorded session here: [Exploring Composer Playground (logianalytics.com)](https://go.logianalytics.com/thankyou-webinar-exploring-composer-playground.html) 

The new Composer Playground puts the power of customized dashboards, personalized self-service and embedded APIs in the hands of software teams. Logi Composer’s low-code, out-out-of-the box capabilities mean faster time-to-market for your application and faster time-to-insights for your end users.  

 You may have heard us say that Logi Composer will take your analytics to the next level without a long development cycle, exceed your end users’ self-service expectations, seamlessly integrate, and unlock the true value of your data. With the Playground we can let Composer speak for itself. 

 Join Logi experts Felipe Blanco, Senior Engineering Manager, and Hayley Wilson, Team Lead, for a tour of Composer Playground and its core functionalities - embed options, dashboard customization features, personalized self-service, and other advanced features

* Felipe Blanco, Senior Engineering Manager, Logi Analytics
