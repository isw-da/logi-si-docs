# Logi Spark 2021 Announcements

[![Logi_SparkBanner_V2R1-01.png](https://devnet.logianalytics.com/hc/article_attachments/4407666009367/Logi_SparkBanner_V2R1-01.png)](https://events.logianalytics.com/events/details/logi-analytics-logi-analytics-presents-logi-spark-2021-virtual-conference/?code=spark21)

Hello Logi DevNet Community,

We are one week away from the user conference, Logi Spark 2021! Below are details for our Keynote and Vision sessions – a lot to look forward to on October 19 & 20. You can still register for [Logi Spark 2021 here](https://events.logianalytics.com/events/details/logi-analytics-logi-analytics-presents-logi-spark-2021-virtual-conference/?code=spark21).   

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Keynote: Democratizing Analytics for Business Value Acceleration: Key Strategies to Leverage Your Data | Data analytics for all - that's the message and the goal. The speed of business is no longer sufficient; to surpass the competition, you need analytics that keep up with your data. A culture of data democratization, analytic experimentation, insights discovery, and data storytelling can transform and boost the ROI on your business data assets.               Dr. Kirk Borne—world-renowned data scientist and astrophysicist, top data science influencer and data literacy advocate, and current Chief Science Officer at DataPrime, Inc—identifies key concepts and strategies to motivate and mobilize your workforce to seek, explore, and produce insights from data through analytic thinking. Learn analytics strategies, including Analytics By Design and an Analytics Mastery Scorecard, along with examples of data storytelling from highly successful implementations across various industries. |
| Keynote: Logi Product Roadmap: Invested in Your Success | Unlock what Logi Analytics has planned in product at this exclusive keynote session led by Charles Caldwell, VP of product Management at Logi Analytics. Learn about strategic inputs and how customer-centric innovation drives the decisions that shape our roadmap. |
| Vision: Bringing Data to Life: The Gut, the Emotion, and the Data | Join our vision session as Mico Yuk, Co-Founder, and CEO of BI Brainz shares why we need more than just data to drive a culture of data literacy, data fluency, and data-informed citizens. While the stigma of the gut continues to plague organizations, tune in as Mico shares her view on why both the gut and emotions are required to accelerate decision making, and how using both can lead to a more data inclusive environment.       This fun and eye-opening session will touch on the use of visual action words, visual storyboards, and real-world examples that are guaranteed to change the way you think about data! |
| Vision: Predictive v Augmented: Analytics Strategies for the Future | Join Logi experts as they debate the pros and cons of augmented and predictive analytics, exploring and illuminating the most applicable approaches for variety of use cases. |
