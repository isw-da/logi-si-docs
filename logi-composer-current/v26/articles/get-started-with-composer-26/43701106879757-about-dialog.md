# About Dialog

The About dialog identifies the version of Composer you are using and provides a link to the terms of service. Access by selecting **About**  in the [UI](https://logi-composer-v26.insightsoftware.com/hc/en-us/articles/43701160499853-The-Composer-UI-Menu) .

![view your software version here, log out of your software, or select the link to read the terms of service](https://logi-composer-v26.insightsoftware.com/hc/article_attachments/46242411192973 "About dialog for your software")

Log out of the product: Select **Log out**.

View the Terms of Service by selecting the Terms of Service link.
